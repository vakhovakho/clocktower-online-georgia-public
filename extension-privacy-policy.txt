Privacy Policy for Clocktower Stream and Rate

Last Updated: 14/01/2024

Introduction
Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Clocktower Stream and Rate, a Google Chrome Extension designed to help players to modify UI and rate other players at the end of the game. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information that you may provide through the use of our extension.

Information Collection and Use
What Information We Collect:

User-Provided Information: We collect information that you provide directly within the extension. This may include your MVP choice of other player.
Automatically Collected Information: Our extension automatically collects certain types of usage data when you interact with it. This may include player list, players tokens, reminders and vote history.
How We Use Your Information:

To improve and maintain the functionality of our extension.
To provide user support and respond to your inquiries.
To provide some statistics and benefits as well as the improved UI for streaming.
Consent
By installing and using Clocktower Stream and Rate, you consent to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not share or disclose your information with any third parties, except in the following circumstances:

If required by law or in response to a valid request by a public authority.
Data Security
We take the security of your information seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect it against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

User Rights
You have the right to access and control your personal data. This includes the right to remove all of your data via contacting us.

Compliance with Laws
We will comply with all applicable laws and regulations in relation to the data we collect and use.

Changes to the Privacy Policy
We will not change the Privacy Policy. But if this happens we will notify you when you use the extension.
